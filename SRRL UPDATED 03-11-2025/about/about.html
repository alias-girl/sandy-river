Join Us

We love to welcome new members, contributors, and volunteers. Our membership extends across the globe, and includes people of all walks of life.


Some of us are drawn by the desire to preserve what once was a vital part of the unique 2-foot gauge railroading and economy of the more remote parts of Maine.


Others are drawn by the sheer beauty of our location, or simply the thrill of riding and operating a rail line that once operated and then disappeared more than 70 years ago.


Whatever your interest may be, we would love to share our historic collection and scenic ride with you.
